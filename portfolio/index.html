Finished Projects

I helped start Olin College's Formula team, known then as Olin REVO (Now Olin Electric Motorsports). As the Electrical Lead of a first-year team, I designed and built up the electrical system. We received 2nd in Design at Formula Hybrid and 3rd in cost at Formula SAE Electric

As part of Professor Morrow's research at Olin College of Engineering, I developed the computer network for a sailing project for blind people. The initiative is to provide blind sailors with a support network that provides basic navigation and feedback to the sailor so that he/she can sail under their own direction.
